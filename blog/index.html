About the blog

I had and still have the dream of becoming a writer (not only of academic papers). When the idea arrived, it felt natural to begin when I am older and perhaps wiser.

These notes host leftover thoughts that may be too eloquent—or too strange—for casual conversation. They are a practice ground for writing, sometimes personal and occasionally sharp around the edges.

I write each post within an hour. Most will be in Chinese.
